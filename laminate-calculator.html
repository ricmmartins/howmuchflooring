  • Standard laminate is NOT waterproof

Laminate Flooring FAQ

How many boxes of laminate do I need?
Calculate: room area + waste factor, then divide by sq ft per box. A 12×12 room (144 sq ft + 10% = 159 sq ft) ÷ 24 sq ft/box = 7 boxes needed.
What AC rating do I need for my laminate floor?
AC3 handles all residential use including busy kitchens and hallways. AC4 is recommended for light commercial or rental properties. AC5 is for heavy commercial like retail stores.
Can laminate flooring get wet?
Standard laminate should not get wet — water seeping into joints causes irreversible swelling. Water-resistant laminate handles spills for 24+ hours. For truly wet areas (bathrooms, laundry), use waterproof vinyl plank instead.
Does laminate need underlayment?
Yes, unless your laminate has pre-attached underlayment. Over concrete, add a vapor barrier. Underlayment provides sound absorption, thermal insulation, and minor subfloor leveling. Never double up underlayment.
How long does laminate flooring last?
Standard laminate: 10–15 years. Premium laminate (AC3+): 15–25 years. Unlike hardwood, laminate cannot be refinished. Longevity depends on quality, traffic, and maintenance.
